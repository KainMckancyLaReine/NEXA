What you just saw

The walkthrough follows the exact path a new team takes — three steps to a workforce that’s already working.

Set up your workspace

Name your company, claim your URL, and connect the tools your team already lives in — Slack, Gmail, Salesforce, Notion and more.

Pick your AI employees

Platform

The platform behind autonomous operations.

NEXA is not a chatbot or a workflow builder. It is a digital labour platform: AI employees that read context, make decisions and carry out real work inside your existing tools — supervised by you, operated by them.

How it works

Understand. Decide. Execute.

Every request runs through the same autonomous loop — no human in the loop for the repetitive 80%.

1

Understand

NEXA reads the email, ticket, record or message, pulls in context from connected systems, and works out what is actually being asked.

2

Decide

It weighs your policies, priorities and history, then chooses the right action — or escalates the edge cases that genuinely need a human.

3

Execute

It performs the work end to end: sends the reply, updates the CRM, books the meeting, raises the invoice — and reports back what it did.
